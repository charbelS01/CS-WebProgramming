Semantic Web Primer

The Semantic Web Introduction is a selected collection of academic articles that explore fundamental concepts regarding the history, present condition, and future possibilities of the Internet. This project consists of four main articles. Their coverage includes significant advancements in online development, the importance of web and Internet security, the global transition to mobile-first design, and extensive data on Internet usage in Lebanon and abroad.

Overview

Article 1, "The Web and Browsers Through Time", analyzes the progression of the Internet from the creation of the inaugural web browser to modern versions that prioritize privacy, standards, and efficiency.

Article 2, "Security on the Web and Internet", examines the primary dangers identified in the OWASP Top 10 and presents strategies for their mitigation.

Article 3, "Mobile-First Development", discusses the global trend of prioritizing mobile device design, driven by the increasing prevalence of mobile Internet users.

Article 4, "Internet Statistics: Lebanon and the Region" , analyzes the condition of internet connectivity, bandwidth, and related challenges in Lebanon, both domestically and internationally.
